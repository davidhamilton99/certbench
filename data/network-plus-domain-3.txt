Question: An SNMP Inform message differs from an SNMP Trap in which important way?
A) Informs are sent by the manager to the agent; Traps are sent by agents to the manager
B) Informs require acknowledgment from the receiver; Traps are unreliable fire-and-forget messages
C) Informs use TCP while Traps use UDP exclusively
D) Informs are only available in SNMPv1; Traps are available in all versions
Correct: B
